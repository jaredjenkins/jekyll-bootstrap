It's like dating: it's time consuming, can be awkward, and rarely results in both parties being totally satisfied. You comb through many candidates hoping to find that perfect match. What's worse is that, it's a very subjective experience. Interviewers can walk away with completely different opinions of the candidate, and visa versa. Finding the right person for any job is hard, but its especially true for tech startups. We're looking for the right person, yesterday.
